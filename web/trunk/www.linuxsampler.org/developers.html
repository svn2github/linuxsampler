Mailing list

To join the development of LinuxSampler, to discuss LinuxSampler development ideas and issues or to send patches please subscribe to the linuxsampler-devel mailing list.

Please note that you do not need to be a skilled audio hacker/coder to contribute. You can be very valuable if you are good at  designing GUIs, have experience with hardware samplers, studio and mixing issues, audio sample formats,  software samplers for windows/mac etc.
